SimTools - Background Music Folder
===================================

Drop your own audio files here and SimTools will include them in the
shuffle playlist on the next launch.

Supported formats:
  MP3  (.mp3)
  FLAC (.flac)
  WAV  (.wav)
  M4A  (.m4a)

Song title, artist and album art are read automatically from the file's
ID3 / metadata tags and displayed in the music player.

To disable the music player entirely, open Settings and uncheck
"Enable music player" under the Music section.
